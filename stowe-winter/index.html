Visiting Stowe in the Winter Time

Recommendations on where to stay, eat, and play.

Recommendations for where to stay, eat, and play while visiting Stowe, Vermont, during the winter.

Hotels

The list starts with the more budget friendly places, then moves up to the more expensive ones.

  • Sun & Ski Inn and Suites: This is where Kirby’s family stays when they visit.
  • Town and Country
  • Hob Knob Inn: Also, a great family place for Après Ski.
  • Green Mountain Inn: Right on Main Street. Walk to shops and restaurants.
  • Edison Hill
  • Field Guide
  • Stowe Mountain Lodge
  • Topnotch Resort
  • Trapp Family Lodge: The perfect place if you plan to cross country ski instead of downhill ski.

There are places available on AirBnB if you’re into that sort of thing.

Restaurants

  • Harrison’s: Favorite of Melanie and Kirby. Reservations recommended.
  • Plate
  • Junior’s: Family favorite. Best Italian food in town.
  • Doc Ponds: A good place for après ski too.
  • Piecasso
  • Von Trapp Brewing Bierhall
  • The Bench
  • Tres Amigo: Stowe’s only Mexican restaurant.

Après Ski

You can also eat dinner at any of these Après Ski places.

  • Matterhorn: Best wings in town.
  • Rimrock’s: Kirby’s favorite watering hole.
  • Cork: For those who prefer wine over beer (though beer is still available)
  • Hob Knob Inn: Great place for the kids.
  • Tap 25

Things to Do

  • Stowebowl
  • Stowe Elementary School: Sledding and playground

Cross Country Skiing/Snowshoeing

  • Trapp Family Lodge
  • Stowe Mountain Resort Cross Country Center
  • Edison Hill
  • The Notch: Drive up Mountain Road/VT 108 until it ends (just pass Stowe Mountain Resort)

Ice Skating

  • Stowe Mountain Resort: Brings your own skates, and skate for free. Rental skates are available.
  • Stowe Arena
  • Edison Hill

For the Craft Beer Lover

  • The Alchemist
  • Idletyme: They have a restaurant.
  • Stowe Cider
  • Von Trapp Brewing Bierhall
  • Stowe Public House
  • Doc Ponds: Large selection of local beers.
  • The Bench: Nice selection of local beers.
  • Tap 25

Nearby Stowe

  • Lost Nation: (Morrisville) Great beer and great food.
  • Rock Art Brewery: (Morrisville)
  • Prohibition Pig: (Waterbury)
  • Ten Bends Beer: (Hyde Park)
  • Green Mountain Distillers: (Morrisville)
