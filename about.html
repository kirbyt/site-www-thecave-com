About thecave.com

thecave.com is my blog site. Who am I? I'm Kirby Turner, a software engineer, author, and snowboard bum.

Since its beginnings in 1996, thecave.com has been a place to find tips on programming, although in recent years it has evolved into a personal blog site where I shares my thoughts, opinions, and rants with anyone willing to read my nonesense.
